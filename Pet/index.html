Adoption Process Pet Decider Community Page

Our Adoption Process page provides a simple, step-by-step guide to help you bring a pet into your home. It explains how to start with a basic application, and understand the required documents and rules—all in an easy-to-follow format. With helpful videos and tips, even first-time adopters will feel confident and prepared to welcome a new furry friend into their family.

Our simple videos walk you through each step, whether you need help with how everything works or the process in which you fill out paperwork! Relax, check out our videos, and know that we’re here to help you every step of the way!
